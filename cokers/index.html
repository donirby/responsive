Jim Reeves was producer for Alvadean Coker and the Coker Family Band. He was responsible for bringing a lot of country artists from Nashville to perform the shows at Compton. Here's Jim now with a medley from one of his performances at the "Town Hall Party."

The Cokers are originally from a ranch in Faulkner County, Arkansas. They moved to Bakersville, California, and made Rockabilly records in Nashville and Hollywood.
